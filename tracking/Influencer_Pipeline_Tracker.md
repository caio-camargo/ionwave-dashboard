## PIPELINE STAGES

1. **Researching:** Identified, evaluating fit
2. **Contacted:** Initial outreach sent
3. **Interested:** Responded positively
4. **Negotiating:** Discussing terms
5. **Contracted:** Signed agreement
6. **Active:** Content in production
7. **Completed:** Campaign finished
8. **Declined:** Not pursuing

---

## EVALUATION CRITERIA

- **Audience Fit:** Do followers match our ICP?
- **Engagement Rate:** >3% is good, >5% is great
- **Content Quality:** Professional, on-brand
- **Authenticity:** Genuine recommendations
- **Cost Efficiency:** CPM, expected ROAS
