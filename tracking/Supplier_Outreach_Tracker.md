## SUPPLIER STATUS DEFINITIONS

- **Researching:** Identified, not yet contacted
- **Contacted:** Initial outreach sent
- **Responding:** In dialogue
- **Negotiating:** Discussing terms/pricing
- **Quote Received:** Awaiting decision
- **Selected:** Chosen as supplier
- **Backup:** Identified as backup option
- **Rejected:** Not pursuing

---

## EVALUATION CRITERIA

### Key Factors:
1. **Price:** COGS per unit
2. **Quality:** Purity, testing, COA
3. **MOQ:** Minimum order quantity
4. **Lead Time:** Production to delivery
5. **Reliability:** Track record, references
6. **Terms:** Payment terms, contracts
