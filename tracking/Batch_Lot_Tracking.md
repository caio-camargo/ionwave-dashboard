## BATCH STATUS

- **Ordered:** Purchase order placed with supplier
- **In Production:** Being manufactured
- **In Transit:** Shipping to warehouse
- **Received:** Arrived at warehouse
- **QC Testing:** Quality control in progress
- **Approved:** QC passed, ready to sell
- **Active:** Selling from this batch
- **Low Stock:** <100 units remaining
- **Depleted:** Sold out
