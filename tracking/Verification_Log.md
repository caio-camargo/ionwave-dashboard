## VERIFICATION LEVELS

### HIGH CONFIDENCE:
- Primary source documentation
- Multiple confirming sources
- Recent (within 3 months)
- Independently verifiable

### MEDIUM CONFIDENCE:
- Secondary source
- Single source
- Older data (3-12 months)
- Reasonable estimate with methodology

### LOW CONFIDENCE:
- Tertiary source
- Assumption or estimate
- Very old data (>12 months)
- Cannot independently verify
