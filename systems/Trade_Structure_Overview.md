# Trade Structure Overview

**Purpose:** How Trades are organized and governed  
**Applies To:** All Studio 3 Trades  
**Owner:** Studio 3 System

---

## TRADE ENTITY STRUCTURE

### Legal Entity:
- Each Trade = Separate legal entity
- LLC or C-Corp (depends on funding needs)
- Studio 3 owns majority stake
- Operator owns minority stake
- VPs may own small stakes

### Governance:
- Board of Directors
- Studio 3 (Founder) has board seat
- Operator has board seat  
- VP (if significant stake) may have board seat or observer rights

---

## EQUITY STRUCTURE

**Typical cap table at formation:**
- Studio 3: 70-80%
- Operator: 15-25%
- VP pool: 5-10%
- Future employees: Reserved pool

**Vesting:**
- Operator: 4 years, 1-year cliff
- VPs: Per agreement
- Employees: 4 years, 1-year cliff

---

## DECISION AUTHORITY

### Operator Can Decide:
- Day-to-day operations
- Hiring (within budget)
- Marketing tactics
- Product iterations
- Vendor selection

### Board Approval Required:
- Annual budget
- Major pivots
- Large capital expenditures (>$50K)
- Equity issuance
- Debt financing

### Studio 3 Approval Required:
- Kill decision
- Change of Operator
- Sale or acquisition of Trade

---
